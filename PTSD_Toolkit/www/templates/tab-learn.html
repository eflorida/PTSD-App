Learn more about PTSD

Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) is a mental health condition that's triggered by a traumatic event.

It can be defined as a reaction to and re-experiencing of a traumatic event that involved intense fear, helplessness, or horror. The person has persistent symptoms of avoidance, numbing, and hyperarousal.

Anyone can be susceptible to PTSD following a traumatic event. It is common for people to show signs of stress-related reactions following traumatic events. However, not everyone develops PTSD.
